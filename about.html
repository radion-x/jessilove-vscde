The Story

From Colombia to Australia

Hi, I’m Jessica. I was born in Colombia, grew up in Spain, and now I call Australia home — my island paradise where I feel safe, welcomed, and grounded.

My journey has always been a mix of discipline and soul. Competing taught me structure; meditation taught me how to listen. JessiLove is where those worlds meet: steady habits, healthy confidence, and a lifestyle that feels like yours.

What I believe

  • Consistency beats intensity — always.
  • Your plan should fit your life, not fight it.
  • Strength includes rest, boundaries, and self-respect.
  • Food is fuel — and it can still be joyful.

A simple rhythm you can repeat: training you look forward to, nutrition you can sustain, and mindset practices that keep you aligned.
